Here we have java script files to run the project.
This project works as a audio visualizer.
In this we have three menus , "Audio Visualizer , Freq Detector , Saved".
The default menu is audio visualiser which visualize the incoming audio and gives the frequency , energy , wavelength of the audio.
Then the recorded is saved in saved menu.
The freq detector menu will detect the audio at a particular frequency and the data is saved if the setted freq is detected.
It mainly helps in space stations to receive a signal at particular frequency and to verify the sound signals at various wavelengths and energy.
